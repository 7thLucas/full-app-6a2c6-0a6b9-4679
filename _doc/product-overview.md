# Product Overview

## Product Name
Pharmacy Management App *(working title — name to be confirmed during onboarding)*

## What It Is
A web application built for pharmacy owners and their staff that centralises drug inventory management, tracks expiry dates in real time, and maintains accurate prescription records — eliminating expired-stock losses and reducing prescription errors in one operational tool.

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Pharmacy owners who need operational oversight of stock levels, expiry exposure, and prescription compliance.
- **Secondary**: Pharmacy staff (pharmacists, pharmacy technicians) who conduct daily inventory checks and process prescriptions.

## Core Problems Solved
1. **Expired stock goes unnoticed** — drugs pass their expiry dates without detection, creating direct financial losses, patient safety hazards, and regulatory violations.
2. **Prescription records are fragmented or inaccurate** — manual or disconnected record-keeping leads to dispensing errors, liability exposure, and audit failures.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Drug Inventory Management** — CRUD for every drug in stock: quantities, supplier details, batch numbers, and reorder thresholds.
2. **Expiry Date Tracking** — automated alerts and a dashboard flagging drugs approaching or past expiry; configurable warning windows.
3. **Prescription Records** — create, search, and verify prescription records, each linked to a patient and a drug; full history, always traceable.
4. **Alert Dashboard** — unified view of expiry alerts and low-stock warnings, prioritised by urgency.
5. **Reports & Summaries** — on-demand near-expiry and low-stock reports for audits and compliance reviews.

## Strategic Goals
- Prevent expired stock from remaining on shelves or reaching patients.
- Ensure every prescription is logged, accurate, and traceable.

## Positioning
Operational compliance tool for pharmacy owners who treat their business as both a commercial operation and a patient-care responsibility. Not a billing or EHR platform — purpose-built for inventory safety and prescription accuracy.

## Brand & Tone
- Professional, calm, compliance-oriented.
- Speaks to pharmacy owners as operators who are also caregivers.
- Language: clear, precise, and trustworthy — never clinical jargon for its own sake.
